Light Saleeg

Recipe Creator

Created by Lina A

Details

Category: Healthy Hijazi

Light Taifi Saleeg.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Egyptian rice
  • Whole chicken (skinless)
  • 3 cups low-fat milk
  • Mastic & cardamom
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Boil the chicken with spices.
  2. Cook rice in the broth.
  3. Add milk and stir.
  4. Roast chicken in air fryer.
