Category: Snack

Dates Energy Bites

Ingredients

  • 250g Pressed Dates
  • 1 cup of Toasted Oats
  • 1/2 cup of Crushed Walnuts or Almonds
  • 1 tbsp of Cinnamon and Cardamom
  • Sesame seeds for coating

Instructions

  1. Mix the dates with the toasted oats and spices in a large bowl.
  2. Knead the mixture until it becomes a cohesive dough.
  3. Form the dough into small, bite-sized balls.
  4. Roll the balls in sesame seeds or crushed pistachios for a crunchy finish.
